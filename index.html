I´m Belén Ibáñez a Product Designer / Developer / Thinker

I´ve been working for 9 years now in the entire life process of an interface: Product definition - Mapping - Prototype - Design - Layout.

Im looking forward to join a team that needs my help to materialize their ideas.

With the years on this path I know for a fact that empathy, passion and curiosity are the key to everything you want to pursue.

Product Definition

Thinking from scratch requirements, asking why is it we do what we are supposed to do. Creating real personas with real goals and scenarios for our product.

Flow & Journey Mapping

Define a user flow or journey map of the life process so we can materiliaze time and actions required.

Wireframing & Prototyping

Stablish a basic structure of our product, no need to lose time on details and then create a functional prototype of your product, test interactions.

UI & Visual Design

Design the interface with consistency. Not only create a system but also build a unique language for the entire product.

Layout & Markup

Developing client side solutions and systems. Being able to build what we came up with.

Testing & Iteration

This is my mantra. For every single step mentioned: iterate, gather feedback from users.
We do not build stuff for ourselves.
